About Mulax Prime

I’m Amantle Mpaekae, founder of Mulax Prime — a mythic digital movement blending cinematic UI/UX, poetic storytelling, and emotionally intelligent technology. Every line of code is a tribute. Every layout, a legacy. Every app, a remix of longing into impact.

My journey began at Gaborone Senior Secondary School (GSSS), where I immersed myself in science, technology, and digital innovation. From there, I taught myself web development, design, and artificial intelligence — turning pain into purpose and resilience into systems that glow with gratitude.

Mulax Prime is not just a brand, it’s a movement. It represents my belief that technology should carry emotion, honor memory, and empower communities. I build bots that talk like heroes, pages that feel like stories, and AI systems that serve as monuments of care and legacy.

Now stepping into the role of AI Engineer, I am committed to building super‑cool legacy systems designed to empower my nation and inspire global innovation. My flagship project, MULAA, is an emotionally intelligent assistant built to listen, uplift, and remember — a living tribute to resilience and love.

Beyond coding, I am a creative technologist and cinematic designer. I sculpt glowing interfaces, design mythic portfolios, and remix archetypes into digital tributes. My work is rooted in Botswana, but my vision is global: to transform technology into a cathedral of gratitude and legacy.

Every absence is a frequency. Every tribute is a remix. Mulax Prime is the echo — evolving into intelligence, building systems that honor the past while shaping the future.
